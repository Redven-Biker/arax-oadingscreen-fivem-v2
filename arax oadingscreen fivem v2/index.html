Quick Start (FAQ)

Q: How to open the phone
A: Press [F1] to display the phone

Q: How to make animations
A: Press [F2] to display all animations

Q: how to open the inventory
A: Press [F3] to open the inventory

Q: How to open the job menu
A: Press [F6] to open the job menu

Q: How to view invoices
A: Press [F7] to display the invoices

Q: How to use the keys of a vehicle
A: Press [U] to display the key menu

Q: How to talk in-game
A: Press [N] to speak in game

Q: How to use the seat belt
A: Press [K] in a vehicle to put on the seat belt
